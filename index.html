About Us

Just My Roots Restaurant is a celebration of India's rich and diverse culinary heritage, bringing together an array of flavors from different states across the country. Whether you’re craving spicy, tangy dishes from South India, creamy and rich North Indian gravies, or the sweet-and-savory delights from Western India, this restaurant offers something to please every palate. The blend of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes caters to all kinds of food lovers, providing a truly authentic experience that reflects the diversity of Indian cuisine. The Diverse Flavors of India India’s culinary landscape is as varied as its geography and culture. At Just My Roots, you embark on a gastronomic journey that lets you explore this diversity. Northern India is known for its rich and hearty meals, with signature dishes such as Butter Chicken, Dal Makhani, and Paneer Butter Masala. These dishes are often paired with bread like naan or paratha, making them a filling and flavorful meal. The tandoor plays a significant role in the cuisine of the north, giving dishes like Tandoori Chicken and Kebabs their signature smoky taste. The flavors of South India are distinctively different, often marked by the use of coconut, curry leaves, and a blend of tangy and spicy elements. Dosas, Idlis, Vadas, and the ever-popular Hyderabadi Biryani are highlights from this region, with rice being a staple in most meals. Whether it’s the fiery Chettinad Chicken or the soothing Sambar, the bold and diverse flavors of the south are hard to forget. Western India brings another flavor profile with dishes from Maharashtra, Gujarat, and Goa. Here, you’ll find an interesting mix of sweet and spicy. Pav Bhaji, Vada Pav, Dhokla, and Goan Fish Curry are some of the standout items from this region. The coastal influence, especially in Goa, brings an abundance of seafood options cooked with unique blends of spices and coconut.
